Bianox

Like I stated before Bianox is a pro at getting huge megacollabs done. Just recently we saw this with Kenos and Zodiac. Bianox's signature style is a very flashy and bright glowy. This makes for some very interesting levels and parts. Lets take a look at his latest level and latest released megacollab.

Zodiac

Zodiac was originally a megacollab hosted by RicoLP. Since its conception it has been taken over by Bianox and verified by Xander. This level features Rustam, Samifying and many more high profile creators. It is also groundbreaking in difficulty standards, as if rated it could beat BloodLust.

Memory

Memory, as the name suggests, is Bianox's modern take on old memory levels. This features a lot of his signature style as it is a solo level. Most of the design is simple, but pleasing on the eye, due to its bright colors. Overall, this is a great take on old memory levels
